About GoldenHarmony

GoldenHarmony is a community initiative dedicated to compiling engaging activities tailored for elders. Our mission is to foster meaningful connections and enriching experiences for seniors in our community, promoting well-being and active participation in later life stages.

Activities

Here are some of the activities we offer:

  • Gentle Exercises
  • Arts and Crafts Workshops
  • Cultural Outings
  • Music and Dance Sessions
